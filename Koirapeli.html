    Koira Peli

    KoiraPeli is a paid commissioned work done in the summer of 2021 together with my classmate, the prototype of the game has only been done and is waiting for possible funding. The game works in 2 phases, you are trying to collect good cards in phase one and then trying to get rid of them in phase 2 and the player who gets rid of them first wins the game.

    Engine: Unity
    Networking: Photon v2
    Hours worked on this: 300+
    My Work: Menu UI, Lobby system, Name and Renaming your player card, Card animations and how they work in photon, game UI
